FAQ

Q: What does "kantan" mean?

A: "kantan" is the word for simple in the Japanese language. I chose this word since it relates to the project (Simple Game Format), and as a student of Japanese I enjoy using the language's words.

Q: Why did you make kantan-sgf?

A: There seemed to be a complete lack of standalone Ruby SGF parsers, and a few of my other projects required such a tool. I finally broke down and wrote one, which turned into a good exercise on Treetop grammar usage, gem building, etc.

Q: When I loop through the game moves it fails. Why?

A: Check if it's a pass move move.include?(:pass)
